- Defaults Local Crystal and Seamless Mode to OFF; Placement Fix, Rapid Attack Fix, and Flotation remain ON.
- Uses hover descriptions and bilingual warnings only for the four timing-critical controls.
- Blocks known or exact high-confidence optimizer/mixin conflicts before KoHs gameplay mixins initialize.
